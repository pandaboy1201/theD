Hundreds Retaliation missiles would be launched from USA and its allies, missiles almost four times stronger than those two dropped on Hiroshima and Nagasaki (two missiles that completely obliterated the cities). The consequences would be unimaginable. Within a span of five minutes the worlds deadliest and shortest world war would be over turning most of the northern hemisphere into a nuclear wasteland.
